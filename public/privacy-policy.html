Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-01-03

1. Summary

Doggerz is designed to work in offline / local-only mode. By default, your dog and settings are stored on your device (browser storage).

If you choose to sign in and enable cloud features, Doggerz uses Firebase (Google) to store account information and (optionally) sync your save.

  • No third-party ads are served by Doggerz.
  • No selling personal data is intended or implemented.
  • Minimal data is collected to provide the features you use.

2. Data stored locally on your device

Doggerz stores gameplay state and preferences locally so your pup persists between sessions. This may include:

  • Dog stats/progress (needs, training progress, journal entries, etc.)
  • Settings (audio, accessibility, preferences)
  • Optional flags (e.g., onboarding dismissal)

You can remove local data using the in-app Settings (or by clearing site/app data in your browser or OS).

3. Cloud features (optional)

If you sign in and enable cloud features, Firebase may process and store information such as:

  • Account identifiers (for example, an email address used for sign-in)
  • Cloud-synced game save data (if you enable sync)

Cloud sync is intended to keep your progress across devices. If cloud is unavailable or not configured, Doggerz should still be playable locally.

4. Microphone / voice controls (optional)

If you enable voice features, Doggerz uses your device/browser speech recognition capability. This may require microphone permission. Speech processing may be handled by your browser or device vendor.

Voice controls are optional and can be disabled in Settings.

5. Location

Doggerz does not use GPS/geolocation. If you provide a ZIP code, it may be used to improve day/night timing and weather-related features.

6. Third-party services

Doggerz may use third-party services (for example, Firebase). These services have their own terms and privacy policies.

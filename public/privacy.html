Privacy Policy

Doggerz Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 7, 2026

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy applies to the Doggerz mobile application (the "App"), a virtual Jack Russell Terrier simulation. We are committed to protecting your privacy while you care for your pup.

2. Data We Collect

Account Data (optional):

  • If you choose to sign in, we may store your email address, display name, and avatar (if provided) in order to sync your dog data across devices.

Game Data:

  • Dog progress (bond, training stats, age, inventory, and similar game state) is stored locally on your device.
  • If you enable cloud sync by signing in, this game data is stored in our cloud database to restore your progress.

Weather/Location Data:

  • If you provide a ZIP code for local weather effects, that ZIP code is used to fetch weather conditions and is stored locally on your device. We do not request GPS location.

Media/Files:

  • The App does not access your photos, camera, or microphone.

3. Permissions

  • Internet: Required for optional cloud sync and weather lookups.
  • Notifications: The App may request permission to send local notifications about your dog's needs.
  • Haptics/Vibration: Used for in-game feedback during tricks (e.g., front flip).

4. Third-Party Services

We use trusted third-party services only to provide core functionality such as optional cloud sync and weather. These services process data only to perform their functions.

  • Firebase (authentication and cloud sync, if enabled)
  • Open-Meteo or OpenWeather (weather conditions)
  • Zippopotam.us (ZIP-to-location lookup for weather)
  • Capacitor (native device bridge)
  • PixiJS (rendering engine)

We do not sell your personal data and do not use third-party advertising or tracking networks.

5. Data Retention

  • Local data remains on your device until you uninstall the App or clear the App's storage.
  • If you enable cloud sync, your synced data remains in our database until you request deletion.
